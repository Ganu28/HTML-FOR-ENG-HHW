Best beaches in Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is one of the most mesmerizing places surrounded by beaches, lovely backdrops and the natural beauty which is sure to take your attention. The scenic views are so scintillating that you can spend hours sitting beside the shores. Also, the weather is just awesome all the year round thus attracting the people throughout the year. If you too are planning to travel to Abu Dhabi then you must know about the different beaches that you must visit. During the sunset, you can spend your time on these beaches and you can find the wonderful vermilion sky with lush green surroundings. Along with that, you can also do some water sports on these beaches and shop for something from the nearest places.

Some best beaches in Abu Dhabi:

Abu Dhabi has a lot of beaches and the outdoor activities, which are quite popular. You can not only enjoy the beaches but try the water sports as well. You will surely have an incredible experience that you have been looking for. But before that have a look at the different beaches that you should visit.

The Corniche – If you are traveling with your family then this is a perfect beach for you. There are 3 gates on the beach and the first one is for the singles and the groups, second one for the families and the last gate for the families and kids. Thus there is not much crowd at every gate and you can have fun to the fullest. Along the beaches, you have a number of restaurants where you can enjoy the finest dining experience. Cycling is a popular activity people get involved into and apart from that, you can also walk on the 4 miles long shore.

Hiltonia Beach club – This is a lovely place meant for the families. You can relax here and enjoy the beautiful views of the beach. Sitting around nature is definitely something that keeps your stress free. Kayaking is one of the favorite activities the people can enjoy on this beach.

Zaya Nurai – This beach is especially meant for the couples or the honeymooners. If you are coming here with your beloved, just head towards the Zara Nurai. The place is less crowded and away from the city too. You can spend some romantic moments on the beach thus proving to be a superb choice.

Al Maya Island and Resort – The next is the place which is popular for friends or if you are going in groups. You can enjoy the views and the different outdoor activities that will make your trip a thrilling one. You have an exclusive DJ pool along with a bar to serve you exotic drinks. One has the option of choosing the sun lounge as well so you can easily go ahead and enjoy fully. Book your flights from Riyadh to Abu Dhabi online and make your dreams come true on this beautiful destination.

These are the top beaches of Abu Dhabi that you can visit. You have many more beaches too so if you have time you can explore them as well.

Best Cafes In Dammam Where You Can Have A Lovely Evening

Dammam | Image Resource : century21saudi.com

A wonderful city in Saudi Arabia, Dammam has a nice ambience and a rich Arab culture that draws many tourists to it. There is great diversity in the culture and cuisines of the place. If you are looking for good coffee to sip on, here are some of the best cafes in Dammam that you can visit.

Derby International Coffee Co., Ltd

You can find the freshest coffee beans being used here to make the best variety that you can sip on and forget all your worries and stress for the entire day. You can step in before going to the office and have a cup in the morning to give you a fresh start for a new day. You can even rest here for some time while returning home from work and grab a cup of espresso to rejuvenate yourself. This prestigious Coffee shop is always open, and you can enter anytime to have a steaming hot cup of espresso.

Dr. Cafe Coffee

It is located at Faisaliah center, Dammam, and serves the best variety of coffee on the planet. You can get 100% Arabica coffee, and an ultimate ambience. As the owners claim, the entire experience is more respectable than the workplace and more comfortable than the home. You can enjoy:

  • Espresso
  • Coffee
  • Tea
  • Mugs
  • Frappe beans along with sandwiches

This coffee shop has some of the best non-alcoholic beverages on offer.

Heart of the Bean

It is located on the East coast of Dhahran, Ash Sharqiyah. This is a café with a difference, as you can get fresh roasted and high quality coffee delivered straightway to your doorstep. The coffee shop has nothing but the best coffee and espresso on offer, made of the best and freshest roasted coffee beans.

Coffee Lounge 85

It is located in Alkhaleej RD at Dammam, Saudi Arabia. The café has become a top spot for coffee lovers in the last few years due to the fast services, friendly and polite staffs and coffee in the greatest quality. Walk in, and you will be amazed at the different coffee varieties that are available.

Pianolla Coffee

This popular café stands at King Abdullah Bin Abdulaziz Road, Dammam, and has the best coffee varieties on offer. You can walk in here and demand just about any coffee variety that you need. The café always stays open, and you can enter here to grab a quick cup of coffee at any time you like.

Sea Pearl Cafe

It stands at Sea Front in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, and remains operational from 11 am in the morning to 1 pm at night. This is a very popular café and its prominent location, savory variety of coffee and friendly staff makes it one of the most visited coffee shops in the place.

South Hills Coffee Arabia

Located near King Fahad specialist hospital and standing at Amer bin Thabet Street, this coffee shop is a winner from the word ‘go’. It always stays open and you can enjoy different forms of coffee that is roasted from the freshest beans.

The city has many exciting and memorable experience, and its rich heritage and history can woo travelers like few other cities in this country can.

It is not difficult to find the cheapest flight to Dammam. There are domestic airline companies, such as Saudi Arabian Air, Sama Airlines and FlyNas Airlines, with the city being connected to other places in Saudi Arabia such as:

  • Abha
  • Riyadh
  • Madinah
  • Bissa
  • Gassim
  • Jeddah

It is rightly regarded as a cultural hub that is home to people of different ethnicities and nationalities. The Top International Airlines include Southwest, United Airlines, Book Airlines, KLM Royal Dutch, Air France, American Airlines, Delta and US Airways.

The King Fahd International Airport is literally situated in the middle of sand, a 50 km drive from the city center. All you can see upon arrival are sand dunes, Bedouin tents and camels on your way to the city.

Enjoying World-Class Shopping Experience at Famous Malls in Dubai

Dubai has made a successful entry in the world of retail stores and shopping malls. If any city had ever made a mark with shopping malls being beyond perfect then that is Dubai. Brands from various other countries emerge in this city to do successful business.

The reason is because there are many tourist attractions that invite travelers from other countries. The shoppers are fascinated with how much grand any shopping experience can be only after they visit Dubai shopping malls.

The climate of the city and the economic situation has come up with such evolution in shopping experience in Dubai. The main attraction being property development, Dubai has rightfully defined retailing and shopping. The well-known and popular shopping malls are:

Dubai Mall

This mall is one of the main attractions of Dubai, situated in the heart of downtown Dubai. It is a compact place having leisure, entertainment and above all retail stores. This mall is the world’s largest mall and most visited mall. There are more than 1200 stores among which you can easily recognize some well-known brand stores like Gucci, Ralph Lauren, Chanel, Alexander McQueeen, Valentino etc.

Mall Of The Emirates

This mall is known to be the world’s first shopping mall. There are more than 630 high-end brand stores among which you can easily locate Boutique 1, Kate Spade New York, Apple store, Centrepoint, DKNY and Forever 21 etc. If you want you can also check out the amazing fashion stores where world-known fashion designer’s artistries are full on display.

This mall doesn’t only include shopping stores but you get to enjoy games, Magic planet, Ski Dubai, skiing or snowboarding for a day, playing with penguins, family themed park, enjoy ski resort, or tobogganing.

BurJuman

This mall particularly concentrates on mixing high fashion brands from over the world and their own popular labels from Bur Dubai. Bur Dubai is the main center of Dubai’s heritage and local businesses. Like other known malls, BurJuman also focuses on other things rather than just shopping. One of the new attractions is 14-screen Vox Cinemas. Some well-known stores are Bvlgari, Charles & Keith, Louis Vuitton, H&M and Burberry.

Dubai Outlet Mall

This mall has a special shopping feature that is to offer 30 to 90% off on purchase on various brands of the stores. Some of the common stores are Fred Perry, Mango and Aldo, Adidas, Coach etc.

Ibn Battuta Mall

This mall holds the name for being the largest theme mall in the world which was inspired by Moroccan explorer named Ibn Battuta. There are six different retail courts inside the mall which you need to discover as an adventure game. Some of the known stores are Geant, Sharaf DG, Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and Decathlon etc.

Other well-known mall in Dubai:

Deira City Centre
Mercato Mall
Mirdif City centre
The Village Mall
Dubai Festival City Mall

Having a Relaxing Tour to Dubai via Flight

There are plenty of choices under your budget to catch a flight toward Dubai. If you are looking for cheap international flights then you can choose from FlyDubai, Air India, Qatar, Cathay Pacific, Air Arabia etc.

If you are going to Dubai and shopping is in your list then it is always better to explore further than the names mentioned above as there are countless options. You can always drive 90 miles away to Abu Dhabi where you can locate many world-class shopping centers.
